Located in the mountainous Hida region of the Gifu Prefecture, is a picturesque town that feels liketime stood still ever since the Edo-period. Visit the morning markets and basque in the tranquility of this well-preserved ancient town.

Renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty, Hokkaido offers visitors a chance to explore lush forests and majestic mountains. In summer, its wildflower fields attract nature lovers and in winter, it transforms into a winter wonderland for snow sports.
